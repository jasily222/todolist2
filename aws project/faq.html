Frequently Asked Questions

To add a new task, go to the "Add Task" page, fill out the form, and click "Add Task." Your task will appear in the tasks list.

To mark a task as completed, go to the "Tasks" page and check the box next to the task you want to complete. It will automatically move to the "Completed Tasks" page.

You can edit your profile by going to the "Profile" page and clicking on "Edit Profile." Make your changes and save them to update your information.

Go to the "Settings" page, where you can select your preferred theme (dark or light mode). Changes will be applied immediately and saved for future visits.

On the "Completed Tasks" page, click the "Clear All Completed Tasks" button to remove all tasks that have been marked as completed.
